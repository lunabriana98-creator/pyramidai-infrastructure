Appendix A: Technical Specifications

Detailed geometric and dimensional specifications for the Phase 1 testable prototype.

Prototype Dimensions (Small-Scale Test Unit) +
  • Base Structure: 6ft x 6ft square footprint.
  • Total Height: Approximately 3.8ft (maintaining the Great Pyramid's ratio).
  • Structural Material: Lightweight aluminum or reinforced composite framing.
  • Goal: Create a scalable, repeatable module for thermal and EM testing.
Geometric & Thermal Parameters +
  • Grand Gallery Angle: Precisely 26.5° from horizontal, optimized for natural buoyancy and laminar airflow initiation.
  • Golden Ratio Spacing (φ): Server racks and component placement will be separated by distances following the 1.618 ratio to minimize electromagnetic interference (EMI).
  • Thermal Gradient: The design is optimized for a strong natural thermal gradient, moving heat from the cool base (Level 1) to the hot apex (Level 4) without forced-air HVAC assistance.
Computational Load Distribution +

Components are stratified based on their thermal design power (TDP):

  • Level 4 (Apex): High-TDP processors (GPUs/Accelerators).
  • Level 3 (Gallery): Standard-TDP servers (Main Compute).
  • Level 2 (Queen's): Low-TDP servers (I/O, Networking, Storage).
